Welcome & Introduction


Greetings, fellow inhabitant of planet Earth. You have arrived at the portfolio of Teagan Penner, which describes her accomplishments over the past eighteen years. Teagan Penner is a graduating secondary student, who - having lived in Bruce County, Ontario most of her life - has gone to the Virtual Learning Center for the past four years. The environment in this school has prepared her for independent learning and self-sufficiency and the course work has equipped her with applicable knowledge. Outside of highschool, Penner T. is the chief of her air cadet squadron, enjoys hiking in the outdoors, draws and writes on her free time, leads two VLC clubs and one other and has done gymnastics over the past two years. She has also been involved with public speaking with both the Legioan and the Royal Canadian Air Cadets. We hope you enjoy reviewing this portfolio.
